# FAQ

## What is Arc Matka?

Arc Matka is a wallet-based card result game with scheduled sessions and published result rules

## Do I need an account?

Your wallet acts as your account. You do not need an email or password

## Why do I sign in with wallet?

Signing in confirms that you control the connected wallet. It helps show your profile, bets and results for that wallet

## Does signing in place a bet?

No. Signing in only verifies wallet ownership. Placing a bet is a separate wallet transaction

## What token is used?

Arc Matka displays stakes and rewards in arcUSDC / USDC where supported by the selected network

## Why are some sessions unavailable?

Some sessions are unavailable because they have not started yet or because the latest session was cancelled. Wait for the next session to open. Open, Close and Midnight sessions follow their own schedule and become available at their set times

## What does Awaiting schedule mean?

It means there is no current session to enter for that market

## What does Session Cancelled mean?

It means that session cannot accept new bets. Wait for the next scheduled session

## Why is my bet pending?

A bet stays pending while the session waits for its card result

## When can I claim rewards?

You can claim when your bet is winning, marked Claimable and the claim window is open

## Can I cancel a confirmed bet?

No. Once your wallet confirms a bet it cannot be cancelled

## What is Jodi?

Jodi is a two digit result made from Open and Close. Open is first and Close is second

## What are 3-card selections?

3-card selections use a three digit code based on the card result values. Codes are checked in ascending order

## Are times shown in my timezone?

Times are shown in your browser timezone where supported by the product interface

## Can results be predicted?

No. Arc Matka is luck-based and results cannot be predicted
